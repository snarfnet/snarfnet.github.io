Privacy Policy

Gratitude Punch - 10000 Karate (感謝の1万回正拳突き)

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Data Collection

This app does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All processing happens entirely on your device.

Camera

The camera is used solely for real-time body pose detection to evaluate your punch form. Camera images are processed on-device using Apple's Vision framework and are never saved, uploaded, or shared.

Microphone & Speech Recognition

The microphone is used for on-device speech recognition to detect words of gratitude. Audio is processed locally using Apple's Speech framework. No audio recordings are stored or transmitted.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Contact
