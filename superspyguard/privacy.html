Privacy Policy - Super Spy Guard

Last updated: June 6, 2026

Snarfnet ("we", "us", "our") operates the Super Spy Guard mobile application. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use this app.

Information We Collect

Super Spy Guard is designed to operate entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Device Sensors and Hardware Access

  • Camera: Used for infrared LED detection, flash reflection scanning, and AI lens analysis. All camera data is processed locally on your device in real-time and is never recorded, stored, or transmitted.
  • Microphone: Used for acoustic anomaly detection, spectrum analysis, ultrasonic scanning, and bug transmission testing. Audio data is analyzed locally in real-time and is not recorded or stored.
  • Magnetometer: Used to detect magnetic field anomalies from hidden electronic devices. Sensor data is processed locally and not stored.
  • Bluetooth: Used to scan for nearby Bluetooth and BLE devices, including tracking device detection (AirTag, Tile, etc.). Device identifiers are processed locally and not transmitted externally.
  • Local Network: Used to discover devices on your local Wi-Fi network via Bonjour/mDNS. Network scan results are processed locally and not transmitted.
  • Location: Used to tag scan results with location information for your scan history. Location data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Data Storage

Scan history and checklist data are stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage. This data is not synchronized to any cloud service and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third-Party Services

This app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. PDF reports generated by the app are created locally on your device and shared only when you explicitly choose to export them.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
