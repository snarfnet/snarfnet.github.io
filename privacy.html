Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Snarfnet ("we", "us", "our") operates mobile applications published on the App Store. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use our apps.

Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to minimize data collection. Most of our apps operate entirely on your device and do not transmit personal data to our servers.

  • Photos and Media: Some apps allow you to select photos from your device. These photos are processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Location Data: Some apps use your location to provide location-based features. Location data is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers.
  • Usage Data: We do not collect analytics or usage data directly.

Third-Party Services

Some of our apps use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: Displays advertisements and may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple StoreKit: Handles in-app purchases. Purchase transactions are managed by Apple. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage

App data (settings, saved content) is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account. We do not operate external servers that store your personal data.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to delete or provide. Data stored locally on your device can be removed by deleting the app.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact
